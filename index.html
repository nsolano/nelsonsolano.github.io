About Me

Hello! I'm a backend developer with a strong passion for technology, software design, and problem-solving. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Electronics and Telecommunications, as well as a Master's in Nanotechnology and Microsystems Techniques, which have broadened my understanding of the exciting world of technology development.

I started my career as a Cybersecurity Analyst and eventually transitioned into backend development. Throughout my journey, I have gained valuable insights into various industries, including education, retail, banking, and non-profits. I am always eager to learn and master new technologies and find innovative ways to tackle complex problems. Additionally, I love speaking at conferences, as it allows me to connect with brilliant minds and often sparks great ideas.

I enjoy collaborating with people who are bold, creative, and passionate about learning. I believe that change is an opportunity for growth and improvement. When I’m not focused on design and development, I love hiking and exploring new places. I’m proud to have traveled across multiple countries and met many interesting people along the way.
